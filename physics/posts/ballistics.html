Ballistics

By Deep Sohelia, 10th March 2016

This toy allows you to simulate a thrown projectile, with variable power and air resistance.
Projectile velocity degrades in both the x and y axes, making the tragectory more akin to those seen in the real world, given the correct value of air resistance.
Projectile color depends on both x and y co-ordinates
