Landing Page Template

By Deep Sohelia, 31st July 2015

This website template is ideal for a landing page that does not need to have much information on it.
This template was designed to look beautiful utilising art from Unsplash and a grayscale color scheme to match. It features an elegant, bold and responsive design. It is ideal to introduce customers to a product, especially when offering a free trial or similar. This is a free template, and you are free to use and customise it as you wish, however, I would greatly appreciate it if the footer of the page remained unchanged.
